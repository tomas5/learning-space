#### transfer columns to rows and vice versa

```vbnet
step 1. select B2-D3

step 2. in the Formula bar type:
=TRANSPOSE(B2:C4)

step 3. after typing in the formula use CTRL+SHIFT+ENTER to apply the same formula to all selected cells
```
